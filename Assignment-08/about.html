I loved making all sorts of stuff out a paper in art class growing up in school. Such as paper claws, fortune tellers, paper cranes, hearts, and many others as well. But I thought that I would share with a you a simple design instruction. And introduce you to a cool website that does custom wallet builds out of an unorthodox material for those who prefer to stand out. I actually used a paper wallet made out of high quality construction paper for about half a year until my parents bought me a really nice one made of leather.
